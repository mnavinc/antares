ABOUT

ASTE is able to provide Small Satellites services to meet customer’s needs, taking care of the complete chain from Mission Concept to Small Satellites Production. ASTE provides the whole end-to-end system, built around low cost, multi-purpose platforms, most of them equipped with collision avoidance system to ensure greater flexibility in orbit maneuvering/control and significant mission lifetime extension.  A wide range of small satellite subsystems are designed by ASTE to satisfy the requirements of our customers.

  • Improvement in the observation and orbit determination capability of Space Debris

  • Develop the collision avoidance systems to avoid the creation of debris.

  • Serves as a neutral international forum for the exchange of knowledge and ideas on challenging issues related to space and space applications.

  • Offers the world unique space technologies and application. Exploration creates new and sophisticated technology; opens up new resources and businesses in space and on Earth; aspiring young people to become the scientists and engineers we will need to tackle our future problems; expands the knowledge of our planet, solar system, and universe; and is the only way to prevent a future asteroid strike on our planet.
